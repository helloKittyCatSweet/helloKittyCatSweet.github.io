Joining Bybit as a QA Development Engineer

A New Chapter: Joining Bybit Singapore

Date: December 1, 2025
Location: Singapore
Position: QA Development Engineer

Today marks the beginning of an exciting new chapter in my career. I am thrilled to share that I have joined Bybit, one of the world’s leading cryptocurrency exchanges, as a QA Development Engineer in Singapore.

Why Bybit?

Bybit has long been at the forefront of blockchain and cryptocurrency innovation, recognized for both strong products and world-class engineering standards. With Singapore’s vibrant tech ecosystem and Bybit’s commitment to excellence, this role feels like the ideal next step.

As a QA Development Engineer, I will focus on:

  • Designing and developing automated testing frameworks
  • Building robust testing infrastructure for trading systems
  • Ensuring reliability and performance of blockchain applications
  • Collaborating with talented engineers across global teams

Expectations and Goals

This role combines my passion for AI, full-stack engineering, and quality assurance. I am especially looking forward to:

  1. Technical Growth

    • Mastering blockchain testing and QA methodologies
    • Building scalable automation frameworks
    • Gaining deep understanding of exchange systems

  2. Professional Development

    • Learning from experts in Web3 and fintech
    • Contributing to mission-critical systems serving millions of users
    • Expanding my network in the blockchain community

  3. Real-World Impact

    • Improving reliability of financial infrastructure
    • Helping secure user assets and transactions
    • Contributing to the future of decentralized finance

Gratitude and Excitement

I am deeply grateful for this opportunity and excited for what comes next. From my undergraduate thesis on blogging systems to full-stack development, AI research, and now blockchain, this journey has been both challenging and rewarding.

Special thanks to everyone who has supported me along the way, especially my mentors, teammates, and friends in Singapore.

What’s Next?

As I settle into this role, I will continue to:

  • Contribute to open-source projects
  • Share knowledge through blog posts and technical writing
  • Explore the intersection of AI and blockchain
  • Stay active in the tech community

More updates from my Bybit journey will be coming soon.

新篇章：加入 Bybit 新加坡

日期： 2025年12月1日
地点： 新加坡
职位： QA 开发工程师

今天是我职业生涯中一个全新阶段的开始。我很高兴地和大家分享：我已经加入全球领先的加密货币交易所之一 Bybit，在新加坡担任 QA 开发工程师。

为什么选择 Bybit？

Bybit 一直处于区块链和加密货币技术创新前沿，以优秀的产品能力和高标准工程实践著称。结合新加坡活跃的技术生态，以及公司对卓越的坚持，这份工作非常契合我下一阶段的发展方向。

作为 QA 开发工程师，我将主要负责：

  • 设计与开发自动化测试框架
  • 为交易系统构建稳定可靠的测试基础设施
  • 保障区块链应用的可靠性与性能
  • 与全球优秀工程师团队紧密协作

期望与目标

这个角色融合了我对 AI、全栈工程和质量保障的长期兴趣。我尤其期待在以下方面持续成长：

  1. 技术成长

    • 掌握区块链测试与 QA 方法论
    • 构建可扩展的自动化测试体系
    • 深入理解交易所核心系统

  2. 职业发展

    • 向 Web3 与金融科技领域专家学习
    • 参与服务海量用户的关键系统建设
    • 拓展在区块链社区的人脉与合作机会

  3. 实际影响

    • 提升金融系统稳定性
    • 保护用户资产与交易安全
    • 为去中心化金融的长期发展贡献力量

感恩与期待

我非常珍惜这次机会，也对未来充满期待。从本科阶段研究博客系统，到全栈开发、AI 研究，再到今天进入区块链行业，这一路的积累让我受益匪浅。

感谢一路支持我的导师、同事和朋友，特别是在新加坡给予我帮助的伙伴们。

接下来

在适应新岗位的同时，我也会继续：

  • 参与开源项目建设
  • 通过博客和技术文章持续分享
  • 探索 AI 与区块链的结合点
  • 持续参与技术社区交流

后续我会继续更新在 Bybit 的工作与学习心得。
