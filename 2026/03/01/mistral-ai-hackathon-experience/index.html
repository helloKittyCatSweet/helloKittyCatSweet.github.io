My Journey at Mistral AI Hackathon - BeWithMe Project

A Special Journey at Mistral AI Hackathon

Duration: February 28 - March 1, 2026

Last weekend, I joined the Mistral AI Hackathon in Singapore. Although my team did not win a prize, the experience was deeply rewarding, and I was honored to receive positive feedback from members of the Mistral AI technical team.

Project Overview: BeWithMe

Core Idea

BeWithMe is designed for people who need emotional companionship but cannot easily meet someone in person. Typical scenarios include:

  • Family members who live far away
  • Friends who are too busy to keep in touch
  • Loved ones who have passed away

Our goal is to use AI to provide a meaningful way to continue emotional connection.

Key Feature: AI Voice Cloning

The heart of the project is AI voice simulation:

  1. Users upload voice samples from loved ones
  2. The system fine-tunes models through deep learning
  3. The assistant responds using similar tone and speaking style
  4. Users get a virtual, emotionally familiar companion

Technical Architecture

Model Fine-Tuning

  • Google Colab for efficient model tuning with free GPU resources
  • Transfer learning based on open-source models

API Service Integration

  • Hugging Face Spaces for mature APIs such as Whisper
  • Avoiding heavy local inference deployment
  • Flexible orchestration of multiple pre-trained models

Data Persistence and Privacy

  • IPFS for storing conversation audio records
  • Blockchain for recording IPFS addresses and ensuring immutability
  • User-owned storage model with stronger data autonomy
  • Privacy-first control over sensitive content

Current Status

The design has been completed, and implementation is in progress. What I shared here reflects the initial architecture and partial implementation of BeWithMe.

Hackathon Takeaways

Technical Recognition

The Mistral AI technical team acknowledged innovation in:

  • Applying AI to real-world emotional support scenarios
  • Building practical systems with open-source and cloud tools
  • Combining technical feasibility with human-centered design

Network and Community

I connected with many talented developers and researchers in Singapore. The technical exchange and career conversations were highly valuable and may lead to future collaborations.

Broader Perspective

Working with teams from different backgrounds expanded my understanding of how AI can be turned into meaningful, real-world products.

Future Plan

  • Complete core product functions of BeWithMe
  • Improve speech quality and conversation naturalness
  • Strengthen privacy and security mechanisms
  • Explore more applications in emotional care scenarios

Acknowledgments

My sincere thanks to Mistral AI for organizing this excellent event, and to friends in Singapore for their warmth and support. This hackathon was not only a technical challenge, but also a memorable life experience.

I hope BeWithMe can eventually provide real support to people who need companionship.

“Technology should serve humanity, not the other way around.”

Mistral AI 黑客松的特别旅程

时间： 2026年2月28日 - 3月1日

上周末，我参加了在新加坡举办的 Mistral AI 黑客松。虽然最终没有获奖，但这次经历非常充实，也很荣幸获得了 Mistral AI 技术团队的积极评价。

项目介绍：BeWithMe

核心理念

BeWithMe 面向那些需要情感陪伴、但现实中难以经常见面的人群，典型场景包括：

  • 异地生活的家人
  • 因工作繁忙难以保持联系的朋友
  • 已经离世但仍被深深怀念的亲人

我们希望通过 AI 技术，提供一种更有温度的连接方式。

核心功能：AI 语音克隆

项目的关键能力是 AI 语音模拟：

  1. 用户上传亲友语音样本
  2. 系统通过深度学习进行微调
  3. 模型学习后可生成相似语气与表达风格
  4. 形成具有熟悉感的虚拟陪伴体验

技术架构

模型微调

  • 使用 Google Colab，利用免费 GPU 资源加速实验
  • 基于开源模型进行迁移学习

API 服务集成

  • 通过 Hugging Face Spaces 调用成熟能力（如 Whisper）
  • 避免复杂本地推理服务部署
  • 灵活编排多个预训练模型

数据持久化与隐私

  • 使用 IPFS 存储对话音频记录
  • 使用区块链保存 IPFS 地址，保证可追溯和不可篡改
  • 强化用户数据自主权
  • 以隐私保护为核心设计原则

当前进展

目前项目设计已完成，核心功能正在持续实现中。本文内容是 BeWithMe 的阶段性方案与部分落地结果。

黑客松收获

技术认可

Mistral AI 技术团队认可了项目在以下方面的创新性：

  • 将 AI 应用于真实的人文关怀场景
  • 用开源与云端工具实现高效系统组合
  • 在技术可行性与产品温度之间取得平衡

社区与人脉

我在现场结识了许多优秀开发者与研究者，围绕技术实现与职业发展进行了深入交流，这些连接对后续合作非常有价值。

视野拓展

与不同背景团队的互动，让我更清楚地看到 AI 从技术能力走向真实产品的路径。

后续计划

  • 完成 BeWithMe 的核心功能开发
  • 优化语音质量与对话自然度
  • 加强隐私与安全机制
  • 探索更多人文关怀方向的应用落地

致谢

感谢 Mistral AI 提供优质平台，也感谢新加坡朋友们的热情支持。这次黑客松不仅是技术挑战，更是一段非常珍贵的人生经历。

我希望 BeWithMe 最终能真正帮助到需要陪伴的人。

“技术应当服务于人，而不是反过来。”
